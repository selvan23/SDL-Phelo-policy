Privacy Policy – SDL-Phlebotomist App

Effective Date: [Add Date]

SDL-Phlebotomist ("we", "our", "us") operates the SDL-Phlebotomist mobile application, used by authorized staff for sample collection and service management.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of data:

  • Personal Information (Staff): Name, phone number, employee ID, login credentials
  • Patient Information: Name, address, contact details, test booking details
  • Health Information: Medical test details, sample collection status, reports (if applicable)
  • Location Data: Real-time location of the phlebotomist for service tracking and navigation
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, app usage data

2. How We Use the Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Assign and manage sample collection tasks
  • Track phlebotomist location for home collection services
  • Process and update test booking status
  • Improve operational efficiency
  • Communicate with staff and patients

3. Health Data Usage

Health-related data is used strictly for:

  • Sample collection and medical testing purposes
  • Updating test status and reports
  • Internal healthcare service operations

We do not sell or misuse health data.

4. Data Sharing

We may share data with:

  • Authorized backend servers
  • Laboratory partners for testing
  • Payment gateways (if applicable)

We do not sell personal or health data to third parties.

5. Location Data Usage

Location data is collected only when the app is in use to:

  • Track staff movement for assigned tasks
  • Provide navigation for home collection

Users can control location permissions through device settings.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect data from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

7. User Rights

Users can:

  • Request correction or deletion of data
  • Contact admin for privacy concerns

8. Access Control

This app is restricted to authorized staff only. Unauthorized access is prohibited.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected with a new effective date.
